# Planning Check

## Project
- **Name**: Bcat Popular Cat Breed Guide
- **Goal**: Build a bilingual English/Korean website that explains about 30 popular cat breeds with credible, detailed, visually rich content.
- **Primary Audience**: Prospective cat owners, cat lovers, families comparing breeds, Korean users searching in Korean, global users searching in English.
- **Core Promise**: Help users quickly understand each breed's appearance, temperament, care needs, health risks, grooming burden, and suitability for lifestyle.

## Grand Strategy Checklist

### 1. Research
- **Market opportunity**: Cat breed guides have durable evergreen SEO demand.
- **Search intent**: Users want breed comparison, photos, temperament, hypoallergenic claims, grooming difficulty, size, lifespan, and suitability.
- **Differentiation gap**: Many sites are either shallow listicles or English-only. A bilingual guide with structured data, comparison tools, and realistic images can stand out.
- **High-value keywords**: cat breeds, popular cat breeds, best cat breeds for families, hypoallergenic cat breeds, 고양이 품종, 인기 고양이 종류, 고양이 성격, 고양이 분양 전 확인.

### 2. Brainstorm
- **Content format**: Breed profile pages plus comparison hub.
- **Language strategy**: English and Korean pages with equivalent structure but localized phrasing, not machine-literal translation.
- **Image strategy**: Use realistic AI-generated breed images only if visual accuracy is reviewed carefully. Avoid misleading users about exact breed standards.
- **Trust strategy**: Include citations, disclaimers, and expert-review-ready structure.

### 3. Monetization
- **Phase 1**: SEO traffic and email capture.
- **Phase 2**: Affiliate links for cat food, litter, grooming tools, carriers, toys, insurance, books.
- **Phase 3**: Breed-matching quiz, downloadable adoption checklist, newsletter sponsorship.
- **Phase 4**: Vet/breeder/adoption directory only if quality control and ethics policy are ready.

### 4. User Journey
- **Entry pages**: Search landing pages, breed profiles, comparison lists.
- **Core flow**: Search intent -> breed page -> compare -> care checklist -> product/adoption guidance.
- **Retention**: Bookmarkable comparison cards, quiz result page, newsletter.

### 5. Pre-Verification
- **Image risk**: AI images may produce inaccurate coat patterns, body structure, ear shape, or unrealistic features.
- **Content risk**: Health and temperament claims need cautious language and sources.
- **SEO risk**: Thin duplicated bilingual pages could underperform.
- **Legal/ethical risk**: Do not imply AI images are real photos unless clearly labeled.

### 6. MVP Scope
- **Initial breeds**: 30 popular breeds.
- **Initial pages**: Home, breed index, 30 breed pages, compare page, quiz-lite, about/methodology.
- **Initial languages**: English and Korean.
- **Initial tech direction**: Static/SSR SEO-first site, likely Next.js or Astro.

## Decision Status
- **Proceed**: Yes.
- **First build priority**: Content architecture and SEO-ready bilingual pages.
- **Second priority**: Realistic image pipeline.
- **Third priority**: Comparison and quiz features.
